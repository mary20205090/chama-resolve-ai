Umoja Demo Chama Constitution

1. Monthly contributions are due by the 5th day of each month.
2. A late contribution attracts a KES 200 fine unless the committee approves a written waiver.
3. A member who misses two consecutive monthly contributions must meet the committee before receiving the next payout.
4. The treasurer must keep a contribution register and share monthly records with members.
5. Disputes should first be reviewed by the chairperson, treasurer, and secretary before a full member vote.
